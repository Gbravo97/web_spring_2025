The Dark Side

The Sith do not ask for permission.
They do not kneel to councils or creeds.
They take what they need, because power is not given—it is seized.

To be a Sith is to embrace passion.
To feel fully. To burn brighter.
Love, rage, ambition—all fuel for the fire that makes you more.

They will call you dangerous. Reckless. Evil.
But the truth? The galaxy fears those who cannot be controlled.

This is not a path of restraint.
It is a path of power—and the will to use it.
Do you dare to understand what it truly means to be a Sith?
